Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly, we collect the information you give us when you create an account, such as your name and email address, along with the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect more than we need to run the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to operate your account, display your expenses, generate spending summaries, and improve the Service. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your expense details for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is associated with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorized access, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party providers for hosting and infrastructure. These providers process data only as needed to support the Service and are expected to keep it confidential. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.example.
